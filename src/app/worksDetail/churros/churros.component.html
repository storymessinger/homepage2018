About the work

This experimental design product was made during the course of ‘Digital design fabrication’, which focused on researches related to fabrication technologies which are becoming increasingly important. ‘Churros printer’ focus on making your own custom churros by using the 3d printing fabrication technology. With this machine, users can actually print out their custom shaped churros and fry it automatically.

The process is as follows.

First, users design their own shape of churros in a dedicated computer program. Then the program translate it into CNC G-code, of which the arduino in the ‘Churros Printer’ recognizes. After the printer prints all the flour dough, the grill plate is lowered down into the oil pot for a dedicated time depending on the amount of dough printed out. When all finishes, user can take out the fried churros and deep it into sugar for a sweet taste.

Structure of the machine

The machine consist of four main parts, of which is syringe part, nozzle part, oil pot and the arduino for control. Most of the part was made out of aluminium profile and 3d printed plastic. Three stepper motors were used to control the movements.

Hardships

The main hardship the project faced during the making was that the machine had a lot of heating problem, cause by the excessive heat while frying the churros in the oil pot. Many work around was adopted like the wood insulation around the oil pot. Also the software programming was also a challenge, which we got a lot of help from opensource in the internet. (Reprap) The basic code for the shape of the churros was generated by python.
