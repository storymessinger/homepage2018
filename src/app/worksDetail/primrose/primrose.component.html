About the work

Evening Primrose is an interactive lamp. The concept is fundamentally based on the Korean novel, where scenery of flowers beneath the moonlight is beautifully described. This lamp let people understand what they lose when they disturb the quiet, solemn atmosphere. When certain abrupt movement is sensed or noise level goes beyond the threshold or light too bright is shone, the flower withers. By this, people can acknowledge that they have disturbed the silent atmosphere and become aware of themselves.

If disturbance is cleared away, flower slowly comes back into life and blooms again. Users can make this process faster by blowing the inside of the flower. This breathes life into the flower.

With its beautiful aesthetic and graceful movement, this product can be used as a silence-keeper in a public place, as well as a moodlight.

Why I chose this work

It is my belief that a product design, especially an interactive product should have poetic aspects in which people could agree upon. This product started out with such intentions, with some exprimental features, in order to make an poetic interactive product., and so is a good example of my design view.
