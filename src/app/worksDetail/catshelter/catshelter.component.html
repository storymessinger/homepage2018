About the work

This product is a small shelter for the stray cats, placed at the parking space. When somebody parks the car over the product, the car’s leftover heat is transferred to the inside of the shelter, which keeps stray cats warm and help them withstand the harsh, cold winter weather.

Every winter, vast number of cats die just because they cannot find a decent shelter to avoid the shear cold weather. As the heat source to take refuge is so scarce, cats are often found to take refuge inside the bonnet of the car. which is the place where leftover heat of the car could be found. For a wayfarer, a warm shelter in the cold winter night is a joyful sight. ‘Cat- Shelter’ started out from this idea; Why not make a shelter for the cats to take refuge?

Stray cats are so vulnerable creatures out in the cold. If people can save them just by simply parking the car in the right place, it is certain many people will be delighted to join and help. By this product, it would not just warm the cats but probably also warm our hearts too.

This product concept has been nominated as a finalist for Sparks award in 2015.
