About the work

This is an app design for students who live alone, named ‘banban’. The target user, a student, will use this application when he wants to order a food but not cannot find a friend who can order it with him. Because food delivery services usually have minimum amount of food for customers to order, one usually cannot order only one person’s amount of food. This application solves such problem by giving users an option to order a chicken with people living around the same area.

When you go inside the application, you can easily find people who live nearby who is waiting for another person to order some food with them. Through an easy matching system, user can group together and order the food to their place. They can then split the food between themselves.
