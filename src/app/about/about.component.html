I’m a South-Korean designer living and working in Seoul and Daejeon. Currently, I’m working in a designer role in IDIM lab. Until recently, I was studying for the master degree in Industrial design.

I am interested in how design can embrace constantly advancing technologies as well as complicated human behaviors, and solve problems we face today in our everyday lives. Thereby I hope to inspire and enable the people who use and interact with the results of my design.

Outside of work, I’m interviewing a bunch of interesting people, making visual graphic posters, sketching other beautiful products and working out in the gym. I can also play some drum too.
